Entity Definition
YFS_FREIGHT_TERMS
( CONFIGURATION Table )
This table stores the details of freight terms.



This table is not extensible.

Tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications.

TRANSPORTATION_PREFERENCEChar (2)Specifies the transportation preference sequence in which the routing guide/table should be read. For example, say the freight term assigns preferences in the order of 1.) Buyer, 2.) Seller, and 3.) Others. When the transportation preference is 1, the Buyer's preference is used first. When not available, the next one in sequence (in this example, the Seller) will be used. If the transportation preference is 3, available transportation will be picked without specific order.
