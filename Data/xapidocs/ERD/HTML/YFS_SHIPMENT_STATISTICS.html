Entity Definition
YFS_SHIPMENT_STATISTICS
( TRANSACTION Table )
This table stores Shipment statistics of a Warehouse on a daily basis. The data stored here is used to render the Daily Shipment Report.

Records purged from this table are archived into YFS_SHIPMENT_STATISTICS_H

This table is not extensible.

Tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications.
