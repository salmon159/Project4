Entity Definition
YFS_IBA_RESV_DEMAND_VW
( TRANSACTION Table )
The view formed by joining the YFS_ORDER_HEADER, YFS_ORDER_LINE, and YFS_ORDER_LINE_RESERVATION tables. This view represents the demands of the order line reservations, which will be processed by the Item-Based Allocation agent.



This table is not extensible.

Tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications.
