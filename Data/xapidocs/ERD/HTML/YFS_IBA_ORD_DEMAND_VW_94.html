Entity Definition
YFS_IBA_ORD_DEMAND_VW_94
( TRANSACTION Table )
The view formed by joining the YFS_ORDER_HEADER, YFS_ORDER_LINE, YFS_ORDER_LINE_SCHEDULE, and YFS_ORDER_RELEASE_STATUS tables. This view represents the demands of the orders, which will be processed by the Item-Based Allocation agent.



This table is not extensible.

Tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications.
