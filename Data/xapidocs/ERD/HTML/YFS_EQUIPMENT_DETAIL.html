Entity Definition
YFS_EQUIPMENT_DETAIL
( MASTER Table )
An equipment may have more than a placeholder location associated with it. For example, a cart may have one or more locations to indicate stock in various bins. For example, left top, right bottom etc. Another example is a sortation equipment that has more than a lane associated with it.



This table is not extensible.

Tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications.
