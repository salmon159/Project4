Entity Definition
PWD_POLICY
( CONFIGURATION Table )
The password policy table contain attributes about the policy. Users can be assigned a password policy that will control when their password needs to be changed, and the required characteristics of the new policy.



This table is not extensible.

Tables should be accessed only through exposed APIs and Business Intelligence. Accessing them directly may result in issues during application of hot-fixes, service packs, and during upgrade to future releases of applications.
