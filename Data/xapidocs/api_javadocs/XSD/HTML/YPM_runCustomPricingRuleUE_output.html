YPM_RULE_VALID The coupon is valid. None
YPM_RULE_INVALID The coupon does not exist. None
YPM_RULE_NOT_ACTIVE The coupon is not active. PricingStatus of the coupon
YPM_RULE_NOT_YET_EFFECTIVE The coupon is not yet active. StartDateActive of the coupon
YPM_RULE_EXPIRED The coupon is expired. EndDateActive of the coupon
YPM_RULE_BAD_CUSTOMER The coupon is not assigned to the CustomerID CustomerID passed in the input. A blank CustomerID indicates that the coupon has no assignments with void CustomerID for the SelectionCriteria.
YPM_RULE_NOT_ASSIGNED_TO_SELLER The coupon is not assigned to the SellerOrganizationCode SellerOrganizationCode passed in the input. A blank SellerOrganizationCode indicates that the coupon has no assignments with void SellerOrganizationCode for the SelectionCriteria.

A blank attribute value indicates that the coupon has no assignments with void value for the SelectionCriteria of that customer attribute.
YPM_RULE_TARGET_SKU_MISSING The item on which this coupon applies adjustments does not exist in this order TargetItemID and TargetUnitOfMeasure of the coupon.
YPM_RULE_BAD_MIN_NO_OF_SHIPMENTS This coupon does not apply as the MinimizeNumberOfShipments flag on the coupon does not match of that in the order. MinimizeNumberOfShipments flag of the rule associated with the coupon.
YPM_RULE_BAD_SHIP_METHOD This coupon does not apply as the CarrierServiceCode does not match that of the order. CarrierServiceCode of the rule associated with the coupon.
YPM_RULE_EXCLUSIVE_COUPON_ORDER This is an exclusive coupon. Another coupon has already applied adjustments on the coupons' target*. CouponID that's already applied.
YPM_RULE_EXCLUSIVE_COUPON_ALREADY_ORDER An exclusive coupon has already applied adjustments on the coupon's target*. CouponID that's already applied.
YPM_RULE_CONDITION_NOT_SATISFIED The coupon condition is not satisfied. Hence the rule is not fired. None.
YPM_RULE_NOT_APPLIED Even though the coupon is valid and condition is satisfied the coupon is not applied. For example: The coupon is providing a flat fee that is greater than the shipping charge of the order. None.
YPM_RULE_NOT_APPLICABLE The coupon is not applicable. For example a coupon is passed and it is not applicable when RunCatalogOnlyRules is set to Y . None.
YPM_RULE_BAD_PAYMENT_METHOD The coupon is not applicable to the passed PaymentTypein PaymentTypeList from the input. PaymentType passed in PaymentTypeList from the input.
