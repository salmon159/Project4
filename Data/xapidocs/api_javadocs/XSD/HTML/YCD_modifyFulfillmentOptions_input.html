Use this element to derive a new OrderLine from an existing OrderLine. If the passed order line is a bundle parent then all the PROD components that have specified KitQty are also pulled in the derived order. The existing OrderLine can be identified by using one of the following:
- OrderLineKey
- OrderHeaderKey, PrimeLineNo, and SubLineNo
- OrderNo, DocumentType, EnterpriseCode, PrimeLineNo, and SubLineNo

If passed line is a bundle parent line, derived lines are created for all the eligible components existing in the parent order. All the attributes which are passed to OrderLine and its children elements are applied to all the components that are created as part of creating derived line from a bundle parent. If KitCode and Item attributes are changed while creating the derived line from bundle parent, result may be unpredictable.
Schedule information is never propagated from the parent line to the child line.

Both ProductLine and ServiceLine elements must be passed to uniquely identify the lines. If multiple associations need to be made, multiple ProductServiceAssoc nodes should be passed. Refer to ProductLine and ServiceLine elements for further details.
AttributeTypeRequiredTable.Column
Actionstring (maximum length: 40) .
Action to perform
Valid Values:
CREATE - Adds a new association between a Product and Service line.
REMOVE - Removes the association between the Product and the Service line. The Quantity for the Service or Delivery line is recalculated. If there are no other associations existing for the Service or Delivery line, its Quantity becomes zero. The options attached to the Delivery or Service Line are also removed if quantity has become zero.
The Modification Types used for adding a new Assocation are :

Associate Delivery Line with Product Line - Validates status of Delivery line being associated with a Product line. The maximum allowed status for this modification type is less than shipped.
Associate Product Line with Delivery Line - Validates status of Product line being associated with a Delivery line. The maximum allowed status for this modificaiton type is less than scheduled. A scheduled Product line cannot be associated with a delivery line.
Associate Service Line with Product Line - Validates status of Service lines being associated with a Product line. The maximum allowed status for this modification type is less than shipped.
Associate Product Line with Service Line - Validates status of Product lines being associated with a Service line. The maximum allowed status for this modification type is less than shipped.

When a Product and Delivery lines association is created, the first two modification types specified above are used to validate the statuses of Product and Delivery lines respectively. For association between Product and Service line, the next two modification types are used.

The Modification Types used for removing an association are:

Remove Delivery Line From Product Line Association - Validates the status of a delivery line before removing the association. The maximum allowed status for a delivery line for association removal is less than Scheduled.
Remove Service Line From Product Line Association - Validates the status of a Service line before removing the association. The association can be removed even after Service line has been completed, that is, the association can be removed even if the Service line has been shipped.

For validations performed while modifying a ProductServiceAssociation, refer to createOrder api documentation.
